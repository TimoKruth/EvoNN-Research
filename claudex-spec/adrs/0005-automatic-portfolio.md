# ADR-0005 — Automatic Portfolio with Expert Override

**Status:** Accepted

## Decision

The default product experience profiles the task, establishes baselines, runs probes, allocates budget across eligible distinct strategies, adapts allocation, and synthesizes candidates. Experts may inspect and override the plan.

## Consequences

The user interacts with one model-building product rather than choosing a research engine. Planner rationale and allocation events are mandatory. Engine internals remain independent.
